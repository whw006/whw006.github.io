NRG-HN004 Eligibility Criteria Per Section 3.2.3

Patients can qualify for NRG HN004 by meeting one of the three sets of criteria detailed below.

  • Age≥ 70 with moderate to severe comorbidity or vulnerability to cisplatin, defined as having any one of the following conditions within 30 days prior to registration:
    • Charlson Comorbidity Index ≥ 1
    • ACE-27 Index ≥ 1
    • GCE ωpfs < 0.6  
    • G-8 score ≤ 14
    • CARG Toxicity Score ≥ 30%
    • CIRS-G Score ≥ 4

 

—OR—

 

  • Age < 70 with severe comorbidity or vulnerability to cisplatin, defined as having two or more of the following conditions within 30 days prior to registration
    • Charlson Comorbidity Index ≥ 1
    • ACE-27 Index ≥ 1
    • GCE ωpfs < 0.6  
    • G-8 score ≤ 14
    • CARG Toxicity Score ≥ 30%
    • CIRS-G Score ≥ 4

 

—OR—

 

  • Any age with an absolute or relative contraindication to cisplatin, defined as one or more of the following within 30 days prior to registration:
    • Creatinine clearance (CC) < 60 cc/min
      • For this calculation, use the Cockroft-Gault formula:
      • CC = 0.85*(If female) * ((140 – Age in years) / (Serum Creatinine)) * (Weight in kg/ 72)
    • Zubrod performance status 2 prior to registration
    • Pre-existing peripheral neuropathy grade ≥ 1 (Clinical or diagnostic observations or symptoms consistent with peripheral motor and or sensory neuropathy)
    • History of hearing loss, defined as either:
      • Existing need of a hearing aid OR
      • ≥25 decibel shift over 2 contiguous frequencies on a pretreatment hearing test
